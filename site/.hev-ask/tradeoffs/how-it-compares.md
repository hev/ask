A comparison table of hev ask against Pagefind, Algolia DocSearch, and Orama across retrieval, AI ranking, deep links, and hosting, followed by a short recommendation: choose Pagefind for excellent keyless static keyword search, Algolia for a managed crawler-and-dashboard service, Orama for client-side vector search you manage embeddings for, and hev ask when your docs are a folder of markdown on any supported framework, you want section-level deep links, and you want a reader's question rather than just their keywords to find the right section.
