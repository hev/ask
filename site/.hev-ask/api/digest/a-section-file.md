A worked example of one section file: frontmatter carries the title, heading, group, order, source URL and anchor, distinctive terms, content hash, mode, verbatim facts, and provenance, while the first paragraph is the summary, the rest of the body is the full read, and the frontmatter arrays are the facts. One file thus serves all three rungs of the disclosure ladder.
