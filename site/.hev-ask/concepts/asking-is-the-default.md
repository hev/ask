The overlay is ask-first: a single word is a keyword lookup answered instantly from the index, but the moment the query grows past one word it switches to ask mode and Enter sends the question to the loop, and on open it shows baked-in suggested questions to make asking obvious. None of this is forced, since a reader can flip to keyword-only so a space just searches for a phrase and the model is never called.
