The build distils each section into a small markdown-plus-frontmatter file and writes the whole tree as a committed, reviewable mirror of your doc paths, where the body is the distilled prose and the frontmatter carries the title, citation URL and anchor, verbatim facts, sources, terms, and content hash, alongside per-term glossary files and a meta file. There is no committed JSON, so prose and facts change together in one reviewable diff; a listing returns only titles, so page titles come from your collection while subsection titles are synthesized into descriptive one-liners and the literal anchor text is kept separately, making good titles what makes the ladder worth climbing.
